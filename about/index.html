// AboutMe()

Hey, I'm Jonathan! I'm a technical designer and current 4th year game design student at Sheridan College. I love tinkering with new ideas and tackling all the logic and implementation challenges that I encounter along the way. I strive to push my technical and creative boundaries every day to ultimately create things that are satisfying to interact with.

Games have been my safe space since I was a child. To this day, I remember the excitement I used to feel rushing home from school, ready to boot up my PC to grind Maplestory and Runescape. That passion stuck with me so deeply that after earning a degree in Kinesiology, I decided to pivot and pursue a career in Game Design!

As I continue my journey in game development, my goal is to create experiences that offer the same thrill that first drew me to games. Whether it's through immersive worldbuilding, unique gameplay, or satisfying movement, I just want to make something that lets players escape into something special, even if just for a little while.
